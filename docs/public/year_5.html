Introduces the fundamentals of artificial intelligence, including machine learning techniques, reasoning, and knowledge representation.

PROJECTS II

Continues developing skills in managing and executing IT projects, with an emphasis on planning, monitoring, and controlling real-world projects.

Centers on creating and managing knowledge-based systems, including the acquisition, representation, and use of knowledge in intelligent applications.

Involves conducting research or development work in the field of computer or mathematical engineering, integrating and applying the knowledge acquired throughout the degree.

Elective courses that enable students to deepen their knowledge in specific areas of interest or complement their training.

ABOUT ME
MATHEMATICAL ENGINEERING + COMPUTER SCIENCE:

PURPOSE

  • Main Goal: Train professionals to integrate advanced mathematics with technology to solve complex problems and drive innovation.
  • Key Focus Areas:
    • Artificial Intelligence
    • Data
    • Cybersecurity
    • Reality
  • Skills Developed:
    • Application of mathematical models in technological solutions
    • Design and development of advanced systems
    • Data transformation into impactful applications

STRUCTURE

Subjects can be taught both in English or in Spanish

The degree is divided in 5 academic courses: First four mostly dedicated to M.E. and the final one completely focused on C.S.

  • First year: Provides basic knowledge for upcoming subjects
  • Second year: Expands subjects from first year
  • Third year: Introduces new fields of knowledge
  • Fourth year: Ends Mathematical-Engineering formation
  • Fifth year:
    • Fully dedicated to Computer Science
    • Bachelor Tesis is done
